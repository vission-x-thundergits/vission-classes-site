About

Who we are

Vision Public School, formerly known as Universal Public School, was founded in 2023 in Jagdev Nagar, Dilia, Sasaram led by the Founding Principal Mr. Vijay Kumar. The school was established with the vision of delivering quality education to the local community.

  • छात्रों, माता-पिता और स्थानीय निवासियों को शामिल करने वाली नियमित सामुदायिक गतिविधियाँ और कार्यक्रम।
  • उच्च शैक्षणिक मानक और विभिन्न चुनौतीपूर्ण पाठ्यक्रम।
  • अच्छी तरह से रखरखाव और अद्यतित सुविधाएं, जिसमें आधुनिक कक्षाएं, पुस्तकालय और खेल परिसर शामिल हैं।
Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality education to students of all ages and backgrounds. We believe that education is the key to unlocking a brighter future, and we are committed to helping our students achieve their full potential.It is an English medium school that trains student for good discipline, moral education, technical education and quality education.
